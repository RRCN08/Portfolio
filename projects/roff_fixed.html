ROFF

Adhesives and Tile Grouting expert from Pidilite

ROFF was one of the projects that I worked during my time as a User Experience Intern at Interaction One Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

They wanted a redesign of their current website to reflect the needs and connect with their target audience, that is the Consumers, Influencers, and Contractors.

For: Pidilite Industries Ltd.

Duration: May, 2020 to July, 2020

Status: Ongoing

Link: ROFF.in

OVERVIEW

Roff focuses on delivering a comprehensive range of tile and stone Fixing and Care products. They also offer a wide array of products in the categories of Building Repair, Grouting Solutions, and Waterproofing and Sanitary Solutions.

I was assigned this project on the first day of my internship. My role was to do research, ideate on the designs, identify the needs and behaviour of the target audience and create wireframes.
